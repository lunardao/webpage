LunarDAO-II is Open

The LunarDAO LIP for DarkFiSquad (LIP-0005) is now live. LunarDAO exists to build, foster & support privacy enhancing tech.

Today with the wake of newly waxing moon, we take a step further down the moonlit path, this time in support of anonymous engineering.

Our second raise starting a new DAO is on Arbitrum and the minimum amount to join is 0.01 ETH. Anyone can participate as EVERYONE benefits form strong privacy. The DAO entry window remains open for 15 more days.
